Customer Base

As a global security and weapon company, the majority of Jake Industries business is with the P.H. Department of Defense and P.H. federal government agencies. In addition, Stark (a Jake Industries Company) provides military and rotary-wing aircraft to all five branches of the P.H. armed forces along with military services and commercial operators in 40 nations. The remaining portion of Jake Industries business is comprised of international government and commercial sales of products, services and platforms.
